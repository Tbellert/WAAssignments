Can ducks see color? Can ducks see in the dark?

Ducks can see color. They can vibrantly perceive reds, greens, yellows, blues color and have excellent daylight vision. But ducks are terrible in light-sensing at night and processing the color in the dark. That’s why they can’t see in the dark.

For this, it is easier to prey on ducks at night.
